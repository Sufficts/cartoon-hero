About Nobara

Nobara Kugisaki is a vibrant and fierce character from the anime and manga series "Jujutsu Kaisen." With her striking appearance and sassy personality, she quickly became a fan favorite. Nobara's weapon of choice, a hammer and nails, combined with her curse technique, make her a formidable and resourceful fighter in battles against curses.
